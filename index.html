What is agile?

Agile software development includes various approaches to software development, in which requirements and solutions develop through the collaboration of self-organizing and cross-functional teams and their customers.

Why is it better?

Agile anticipates change and allows for much more flexibility than traditional methods. It advocates adaptive planning, evolutionary development, early delivery and continuous improvement and promotes flexible responses to changes
